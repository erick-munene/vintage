About

Vintage is a car show that started 3 years ago in Nairobi , Kenya where owners of classic cars met. The event focuses on antique cars mostly the pre -90s generation of automobiles. Ever since the first meeting the club has grown and expanded to competitions.

Muscle Car

This club allows muscle car owners to meet and compare their cars. It also allows them to participate in various muscle car competition such as drag racing and burnouts..

Classic Cars

All off those who are passionate about classic cars, Vintage and modern are able to meet in this club and interact. There are competitions that check for the best classical car out there which are held annually..

Modern Cars

We are planning on introducing a new club that focusses only on new car models.They will deal with performance,safety and design.

Experimental

We also have the experimental group where all those who like to customize different models of cars are able to meet. We offer competitions for the most innovative vehicles out there.
